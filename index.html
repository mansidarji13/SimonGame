HOW TO PLAY

  1. Press any key on your keyboard to start the game.
  2. Watch the sequence of flashing colors and remember them.
  3. Repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons in the exact same order.
  4. Every time you succeed, the game adds one more step to the pattern.
  5. Clicking the wrong color triggers "Game Over". Press any key to restart!
